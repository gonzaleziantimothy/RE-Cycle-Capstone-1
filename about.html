Our Story

Re Cycle is a home grown bicycle repair and restoration workshop. Seeing old bicycles come to waste became an inspiration for owner, Tim Gonzalez, to offer bike repair and services in his own garage. The company is dedicated in reviving old, worn out bicycles and bring them back to life. It caters to all types of bicycles, believing that every bicycle deserve to be on the road.

Our Mechanics

Our mechanics are highly skilled and very professional when it comes to handling your precious metal. All of our mechanics was sent to Bike School Asia, to undergo a rigorous Professional Bike Mechanic training program. This is to ensure that they can take care of your sweet ride.
