Repair

Almost all the parts of the bike can be fixed when it's broken. We do minor repairs and parts replacement for your beloved bike to make it run again.

Restore

Your grandfathers bicycle that is laying around in your garage has great potential. Bring it to us and we can turn it bike to life, pun intended. We do simple to finely detailed bicycle restorations.

We are driven by perfection and precision. We take a classic bicycle in any state of decline or disrepair and restore it. Every part, every bolt, every cable to its original, magical splendour. We take great pride in every detail of the beautiful marques we restore whether the end result is for play or purely for investment. Check out our previous projects, you might have one laying around somewhere.

Maintain

Your bike also needs tender loving care and we can do it for you. We provide bike wash, greasing and wheel truing.

Take your bike to us before that weekend group ride and we can make it shiny and shimmering.
